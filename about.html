WE ARE CYBERNETICS

Cyber what?

The Cybernetics Association under the Department of Computer Science at St. Joseph's University was set up to promote a sense of fraternity and belonging among under graduate students who share a flair for Computer Science. The association's primary goal is to provide computer enthusiasts with a venue for exchanging and enhancing their ideas through events, activities, and seminars, and thereby enhancing their expertise in the rapidly evolving world of technology.
